Who We Are

BLite: 20+ Years of Nutrition Expertise in the UAE

Eat Light, Live Bright

BLite is a trusted pioneer in the UAE’s health and wellness industry, with over two decades of experience helping people lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Formerly known as Slim’n Lite, BLite specializes in customized nutrition programs, chef-crafted meals, and professional dietary support from licensed clinical dietitians. Whether you're aiming for weight loss, muscle gain, or managing conditions like diabetes, hypertension, or PCOS, we provide personalized meal plans designed for long-term success.

As part of NCC Holding, we combine clinical nutrition expertise with gourmet cooking to deliver balanced, delicious meals that fit seamlessly into your lifestyle. Every program is tailored to your health goals, ensuring convenience, sustainability, and measurable results.

At BLite, we make healthy eating simple and enjoyable—with science-backed strategies, compassionate care, and premium service.
