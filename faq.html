It is always preferable to remove the food from the bag or box and place all items in the fridge immediately. If you are away from home, the food will remain safe in your cooler bag at room temperature (not in the external heat) for up to a maximum of 3 hours, with ice packs. If you choose to receive your meals in a carton box, food should be transferred immediately to the refrigerator.
Food should be removed from the fridge and heated either on a pan, oven, sandwich grill or microwave. Lunch and soups containers are microwavable while other containers have to be emptied in another microwaveable container for reheating.
